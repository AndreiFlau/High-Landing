How much does a Teams subscription cost?

Contributors are members of your team who need access to use the Mac app to create and edit Sketch documents. These prices don’t include sales tax, which may still apply. Head over to our pricing page to get full details.

Do I still need to purchase
licenses for the Mac app?

No, you do not need to purchase separate licenses for the Mac app. Your Teams subscription covers access to the app for all contributors on your team. You can simply download it and start using it after subscribing to Teams.

What are the differences between Contributors and Viewers?

Contributors have full access to the Sketch documents in your shared workspace, including creating, editing, and updating the content. Viewers, on the other hand, can only see the documents in read-only mode. Viewers cannot make changes to shared files.

How long does the free Teams subscription trial last?

Our free Teams trial gives you full access to all Teams features for a period of 14 days. This should give you ample time to fully test our platform and see if it is right for your organization. You can always upgrade at any time if you wish to keep the full experience.

Will program for Teams replace volume licensing?

The program for Teams is intended to be an eventual replacement for volume licensing. The goal is to provide our users with a simpler way to maintain licenses. Existing volume licenses will continue to work, and you will be informed as the replacement transition occurs, but we believe that the program for Teams will make things easier for everyone.
